# FAR-Based RFP

## Evaluator mindset
Section M criteria are the rubric. Scorers read your volumes looking for the exact words Section L asked for. Theme statements at the top of each section + an action caption under every graphic are non-negotiable.

## Required artifacts
- Compliance matrix covering every "shall" in L/M/PWS/SOW
- Technical volume (architecture, approach, schedule, risk)
- Management volume (PMP, staffing, QA, IMS)
- Past Performance volume (3-5 PPQs, CPARS, relevancy mapping)
- Cost volume (CLINs, BOEs, direct/indirect rates, assumptions)
- Reps & Certs (FAR 52.204-X, cybersecurity, small-biz status)

## Common pitfalls
- Merging Section L (instructions) and Section M (evaluation) — treat them separately
- Over-promising in narrative without CLIN coverage in cost
- Generic PP narratives not tied to Section M sub-criteria
- Missing CDRL deliverables in the SOW-to-section crosswalk
