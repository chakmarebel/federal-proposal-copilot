# Sources Sought Response

## Evaluator mindset
"Can we set this aside for small business? Are there 2+ qualified SBs? What's the right NAICS?"

## Required artifacts
- Company identification (CAGE, UEI, NAICS codes, size status)
- Small-biz category assertions (SDVOSB, WOSB, 8(a), HUBZone as applicable)
- Relevant experience (3-5 examples — contract #, customer, scope, value, period)
- Capability summary mapped to the stated requirement
- Interest statement (plan to bid, teaming preferences)

## Common pitfalls
- Treating it like a pitch (it's not — it's a market research form)
- Claiming capabilities without example contracts to back them
- Missing the NAICS / size-standard section (the point of the exercise)
- Too long — 3 pages is often ideal, 5 is the ceiling
