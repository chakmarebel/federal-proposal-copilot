# IDIQ Task Order Response

## Evaluator mindset
Evaluator is an existing customer or CO under the base contract. They know your company. They care about: can you execute this specific task, on schedule, with named staff, at the proposed labor mix?

## Required artifacts
- Compliance matrix against the TO's Section L/M (inherit base-contract clauses by reference)
- Technical approach scoped to the TO SOW
- Staffing plan with named key personnel + resumes
- Relevant experience under this or similar vehicles (not full PPQ volume)
- Cost volume using base-contract rates unless otherwise directed

## Common pitfalls
- Pasting base-contract boilerplate into a short TO response
- Proposing staff without confirmed availability letters
- Rate build-ups that don't match the base-contract-approved structure
