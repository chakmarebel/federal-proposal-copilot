# SBIR Phase I

## Evaluator mindset
"Does the technical approach show feasibility? Does the team understand the commercialization path? Is the cost reasonable for a feasibility study?"

## Required artifacts
- Technical objectives tied to topic description
- Work plan (tasks, timeline, deliverables) for the Phase I period
- Commercialization plan (Phase III customers, market, transition)
- Key personnel bios
- SBIR budget (DoD DD Form 250 or agency-specific template)
- Data rights: SBIR Policy Directive markings

## Common pitfalls
- Proposing a full program instead of a feasibility study
- Weak commercialization plan (scores are real even in Phase I)
- Budget exceeding topic cap or misallocating direct labor
- Not citing the topic number and objectives verbatim
