# Broad Agency Announcement

## Evaluator mindset
Scientific peer review. "Is the approach novel, feasible, and likely to advance the state of the art? Does the team have the credentials to execute?"

## Required artifacts
- Research hypothesis and objectives
- Technical approach with milestones and go/no-go gates
- Principal Investigator bio + key personnel credentials
- Prior research results (publications, prototypes)
- Schedule with phases and decision points
- Cost volume (usually FAR-compliant even under BAA)

## Common pitfalls
- Pitching a product instead of a research plan
- Over-selling TRL (claiming 7 when it's a 4)
- Omitting PI credentials — merit review is personality-driven
- Treating milestones as deliverables instead of decision gates
