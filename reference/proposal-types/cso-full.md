# CSO Full Proposal (Phase 2)

## Evaluator mindset
Team is already interested (you passed Phase 1). Now they need to justify award and structure the deal. Give them the language to do both.

## Required artifacts
- Expanded technical approach
- Execution plan with milestones and acceptance criteria
- Commercial-item pricing with market rate references
- Data rights assertions (if IP is in scope)
- Relevant experience (not full PPQ)
- Proposed terms: IP, warranty, delivery, payment
