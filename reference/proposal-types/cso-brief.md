# CSO Solution Brief (Phase 1)

## Evaluator mindset
"Is this a commercial capability that solves a named area of interest, and is it worth inviting to Phase 2?" Not a cost competition — a concept competition.

## Required artifacts
- Problem → solution → outcome narrative (no FAR boilerplate)
- One architecture graphic
- ROM cost range with 2-3 key assumptions
- Delivery timeline (weeks/months, not quarters)
- Commercial-item justification (FAR 2.101 if referenced)

## Common pitfalls
- Writing it like a FAR proposal — too long, too formal, too priced
- Burying the capability on page 4 after company history
- Claiming commercial-item status without evidence of non-government sales
