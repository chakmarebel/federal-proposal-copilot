# OTA White Paper

## Evaluator mindset
"Is this a prototype worth funding under 10 USC 4022? Does the team include non-traditional contributors? Can they execute fast?"

## Required artifacts
- Prototype concept + technology maturity claim
- Expected outcomes and transition path (follow-on production via 10 USC 4022(f))
- Team composition with non-traditional designation
- ROM range with key cost drivers
- Schedule in months with clear milestones
- Consortium affiliation (if applicable)
