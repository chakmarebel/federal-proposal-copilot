## Background

The Joint Expeditionary Directorate supports units operating in Denied, Disrupted, Intermittent, and Limited (DDIL) environments. Current AI tools assume persistent cloud connectivity and cannot operate in contested or disconnected scenarios. This CSO seeks commercial generative AI solutions suitable for on-device deployment in expeditionary conditions.

## Areas of Interest

JED is particularly interested in commercial solutions that demonstrate the following capabilities:

**AoI-1: On-Device Inference.** Solutions must perform generative AI inference (text generation, summarization, Q&A) on standard ruggedized edge hardware without requiring cloud or enterprise connectivity.

**AoI-2: Disconnected Operation.** Solutions must maintain full functionality during extended periods (≥14 days) of zero connectivity with no degradation of core capabilities.

**AoI-3: Model Specialization.** Solutions must support rapid fine-tuning or adapter-based specialization for mission-specific use cases (planning, ISR summarization, reporting) within 90 days of mission tasking.

**AoI-4: Security Posture.** Solutions must demonstrate a path to operating on networks at the IL5 or higher Impact Level, including on-device handling of CUI without ephemeral cloud dependencies.

## Evaluation Criteria

Submissions will be evaluated against the following criteria, in order of importance:

1. **Commercial Maturity.** Evidence of real-world commercial or government deployments of the proposed solution in comparable operational environments. Paper designs are not competitive.

2. **Coverage of Areas of Interest.** Breadth and depth of coverage across AoI-1 through AoI-4. Depth in one area does not offset absence in another.

3. **Path to Deployment.** Clarity of the proposed deployment approach, including hardware requirements, integration with existing tactical systems, and timeline to first operational capability.

4. **Affordability.** Total cost of the proposed Phase 1 engagement as a Rough Order of Magnitude, including key cost drivers and scaling assumptions.

5. **Commercial-Item Basis.** Clear demonstration that the proposed solution is a commercial item under FAR 2.101 and not a bespoke research effort.

## Submission Contents (Required)

## Awards

Phase 1 awards are not contemplated under this CSO. Selected Phase 1 submissions will be invited to Phase 2 at no cost to the Government.
